Illuminating the Unused: Harnessing Dark Data for Smarter Project Management
The file Darkdatarisks sheds light on the Hidden Costs of Ignoring Dark Data - Risks, inefficiencies, and missed opportunities. I have hypothetical values based on industry standards to understand the impact of dark data on projects/organisations. The calculations are done to expose the Annual Savings from Cleaning Dark Data, Breach components, Risk, Inefficiency and missed opportunity.

Risk exposure is calculated as the probability delta (breach_dark − breach_base) multiplied by total breach liability (l_breach), representing the expected annual cost of carrying dark data.
Search inefficiency uses fully-loaded hours: t_search_hr × rate × n_team × 52.
Missed opportunities assumes a 30% insight capture rate against dark data records across all projects.
Risk multiplier applies a 0.75 adjustment factor to the raw 2× probability ratio, giving a 1.5× severity uplift (50% worse outcomes).
GDPR fine is modelled at 1% of annual turnover — below the Article 83(5) maximum of 4% — as a conservative estimate.
